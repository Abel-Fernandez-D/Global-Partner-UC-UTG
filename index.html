Word Search Challenge: Financial Education

Find the 10 words related to financial success. Click on a word in the list below to reveal a fun fact or statistic after finding it in the grid!

Words to Find

  • SAVINGS In Peru, only 29% of adults saved money formally in 2021 (World Bank).
  • TAX In The Gambia, taxes fund public services like education, but compliance is low due to limited financial literacy.
  • ASSET Owning assets like land can help young Peruvians build wealth for the future.
  • LIABILITY Debts, or liabilities, can limit financial freedom if not managed well, a challenge for many in The Gambia.
  • BALANCE A balanced financial statement helps businesses in Peru track their economic health.
  • SUPPLY In The Gambia, the supply of vocational training programs is growing to meet job market needs.
  • DEMAND There’s a high demand for financial literacy programs among Peruvian youth (UNESCO).
  • INFLATION Inflation in Peru reached 8.8% in 2022, affecting purchasing power (World Bank).
  • GUARANTEE Consumer rights in The Gambia include guarantees to protect against faulty goods.
  • CLAIM In Peru, knowing how to file a claim can help consumers protect their financial rights.
